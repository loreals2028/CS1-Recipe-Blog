Description:

This Vegan Eggplant Parmesean is a great dish to make for your friends and family. This recipe is dairy and egg-free and skips the extra step of turning it into a casserole and instead serves the crispy fried eggplant slices with a side of marinara.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Eggplant
  • All Purpose Flour

Steps

  1. Slice eggplant into thin rounds slightly less than 1/2 inch thick, and sprinkle each side liberally with sea salt. Arrange in a circular pattern in a colander and place in the sink to draw out the bitter taste of the eggplant.
